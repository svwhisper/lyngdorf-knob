lyngdorf-knob

A physical volume / mute / play-pause controller for Lyngdorf amplifiers, built on the Waveshare ESP32-S3-Knob-Touch-LCD-1.8.

Install

Plug the device into USB, then click below. Works on Windows, Mac, and Linux — the browser handles flashing, nothing to install on your computer.

Requires Chrome, Edge, Brave, or another Chromium-based browser. Safari and Firefox don't yet support the WebSerial API used here. Mac users on Safari can grab Chrome or Edge just for this one step.

If your device isn't shown when you click Install, make sure no other program is holding the serial port open (Arduino IDE, idf.py monitor, etc.).

First-time setup

  1. After flashing, the device starts an open WiFi access point named LyngdorfKnob.
  2. Connect to it from your phone or computer, and open http://192.168.4.1 in a browser.
  3. Enter your home WiFi credentials and your Lyngdorf amplifier's IP address. Save.
  4. The device reboots, joins your WiFi, and is ready to use.
